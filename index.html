Take The Quiz

1.The quiz contains 10 questions and there is no time limit.

2.There are only Multiple Choice questions with only one option correct.

3.Select an answer for every question. Unanswered questions will be marked as incorrect.

4.You have only 5 mins to attempt the quiz.Quiz will end as soon as the timer runs out.

5.You will be awarded 5 point for each correct and deducted 2 for wrong answer.

6.If you want to try to get a better score, click the Try Again button at the bottom of the results page. You can try the quiz as many times as you like

7.At the end of the Quiz, your total score will be displayed. The maximum score is 50 points.

Good Luck!

Note: Quiz and Timer Starts As soon as Start Quiz button is clicked.
